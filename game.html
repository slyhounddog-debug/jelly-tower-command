Field Manual & Mechanics

Defensive Systems

Auto-Turrets: Deal 50% damage/range. Target closest enemy.

Barriers (Shields): Regen 1% HP/s. Enemies deal 10 dmg. Max 5.

Enemies

Jelly beanie: Standard threat. Spawn rate increases over time. Missile HP is calculated as a base of 30, plus the current enemies/min, and an additional 0.1 HP for every enemy killed.

Piggy Bank: Rare spawn (1/min). High HP. Grants massive bonus money and loot on kill!

Combat

Piercing: Projectiles pierce until damage potential is exhausted.

Platforming

Phasing: Press 'S' to drop through platforms.

Jumping: 2 mid-air jumps allow for agile recovery.
